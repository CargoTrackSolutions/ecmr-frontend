4. Description of Service

The eCMR service is a digital platform dedicated to the management, issuance, and electronic signing of transport documents (electronic consignment notes), in accordance with:

  • The Convention on the Contract for the International Carriage of Goods by Road (CMR Convention, 1956);
  • The Additional Protocol to the CMR Convention on the Electronic Consignment Note (eCMR Protocol, 2008);
  • Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 on electronic identification and trust services (eIDAS).

The platform uses advanced electronic seals to guarantee the authenticity and integrity of documents, in compliance with the requirements of the eCMR Protocol and the eIDAS Regulation. Documents issued through this service have the same legal effect as their paper-based counterparts.
